 
                                                   Introduction:
 Our system is a software application designed to help plan, organize, and manage events of various types and sizes.
 It is a comprehensive tool that assists event planners in coordinating all the aspects of an event, from scheduling to 
logistics to marketing and communication.There are some features of our system like event planning, customized processes
and scheduling, budget management,marketing and promotion, speaker and presenter management, logistics, human resources
allocation, registration and ticketing, onsite monitoring,sponsor management.By using an event management system, 
event organizers can streamline the planning process, save time and effort, reduce the risk of errors and miscommunication, 
and ultimately, create successful events that meet the needs and expectations of attendees.
